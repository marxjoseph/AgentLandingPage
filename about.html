About John Marques:

Born in Portugal and having made Toronto, Canada my home almost 50 years ago, I am John Marques, a seasoned RE/MAX Real Estate Agent with over 35 years of industry experience. Fluent in both Portuguese and English, I aim to connect with a diverse clientele and ensure smooth communication throughout the real estate process. As a proud father of two boys and a loving grandfather, I understand the significance of finding the perfect home for every family. Whether you're a first-time homebuyer, growing your family, or looking to downsize, I am dedicated to helping you find a property that suits your unique lifestyle.
